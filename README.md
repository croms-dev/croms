# Cross-Scale Regional Ocean Modeling System (CROMS)

CROMS is an advanced ocean modeling system built upon AI, ROMS, and CROCO, and is collaboratively developed and maintained by Indian and international research groups spanning Earth science, computer science, artificial intelligence, and applied mathematics. A central objective of CROMS is to resolve very fine-scale (few meter) ocean dynamics especially in coastal regions as well open ocean while accurately capturing their interactions with larger-scale processes. The system integrates advanced AI capabilities, flexible hydrostatic and non-hydrostatic solvers, fully coupled ocean, wave, atmosphere dynamics, and dynamically evolving marine biogeochemistry, enabling a comprehensive and realistic representation of the marine environment.
